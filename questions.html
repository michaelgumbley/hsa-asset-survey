Information Asset Survey - Questions

This survey is not optimised for small devices. Please use a desktop or laptop to complete it.

Key Information

What are we doing? The intent of this form is to gather initial information related to HealthServe Australia's digital assets, also known as information assets. We are creating an Information Asset Register as part of our Cyber Security assessment.

How long will it take? I'm not going to lie, it's probably not going to be quick. Get yourself a tea or coffee before you go on. It will probably take you 30 mins or so to complete this properly. It's always easier to update something than it is to create it from scratch.

What are information assets? Any data, device or application used to access, modify or store HSA information. In our case this will likely include device and application assets that HSA does not own or license. (More on that as we go.)

If in doubt, add it. As you answer the following questions, opt to include things that you are unsure about. It is better for us to remove superfluous items later if they are not required, rather than missing important information.

A note on this process: This form is being distributed internally only - directly to staff, board members and volunteers who interact with HSA information. This survey has been authorised by the Board and the CEO, Michael Burke.
